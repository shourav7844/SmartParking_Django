Services

Your satisfied services in your reach

Easy registration

Smart parking system offers an easy registration process for the users. Users can register to the system without any hassle. Admins and users have separate registration setups. The Software Registration process is controlled and managed solely by the admins.

Park smartly

Entry of vehicle is detected and scanned automatically. Vehicles are routed to the nearest possible location using routing algorithm. Various sensors provide continuous data stream about the vehicles. So, overall the system lets you park smartly.

Convenient place

After scanning each vehicle is allocated to a designated place. Routing algorithm selects a place closest to the vehicle. However, if any vehicle fails to find itâ€™s designated place, it is then given with a new place to park. The system provides an overall convenient placing for the vehicles.

About

A short description about our journey

  • March 2022

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our journey started on March 2022. Some projects were proposed but after many discussions we finally started working on Smart Parking System. Over the months gradually we went ahead with our project. And finally on July 2022, we established a system good enough to be implemented practically in a real life parking system.

  • April 2022

    An team is Born

    From the starting of this project we five have been working together like a team. A number of people can be called a group, but not a team. We were all committed to our purpose and tried our best to gradually enhance the performance of the system with true team spirit.

  • May 2022

    Hosted for test

    After many months of scrutiny, we finally established a project firm enough to be hosted for test. We looked at our project from the point of view of practical reasoning. Many edits and modifications were done. Many features were presented as well. Finally the system had the compatibility to go on full on ground service.

  • June 2022

    Transition to full service

    The system underwent many layers of changes and modifications. The final end result is what you see in front of you now. The system is now ready to be transitioned to full service. We hope that the system will come to benefit the faculty, staff and students of MIST in the years to come.
